About game

Mazuri is a massive futuristic city on an island in the year 2040. When everywhere else physical violence has been banned, Mazuri City remains the only place where fighters can practice their craft and make a full living off of it. This is where the player takes on a role of a fighter and embarks on his adventure towards fame, glory and wealth.
